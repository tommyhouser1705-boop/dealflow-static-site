Frequently Asked Questions

How does the service work?

You tell us your budget, origin and preferences. We research flights, stays, cars and experiences to maximise value and minimise cost. You choose your favourite itinerary and we handle the booking (Concierge and Group tiers) or provide links so you can book yourself (Lite and Budget Explorer).

Do you only work with young travellers?

No. We specialise in under‑25 travel because there’s a gap in the market, but we work with travellers of all ages. Our Young Traveller Advantage just means we have extra expertise in unlocking deals for students and young professionals.

Do I have to pay for the trip through you?

Only if you select a Concierge or Group package. For Lite and Budget Explorer, we send you booking links so you can pay directly with airlines, hotels or rental agencies. Concierge and Group clients authorise payment via a secure form and we make the bookings on your behalf.

What if my trip gets more expensive after I book?

Many providers allow free changes or cancellations within a certain timeframe. If your plan includes Flexible Repricing monitoring, we’ll rebook or adjust your reservation when a better price appears.

Which destinations do you know best?

Tommy has personally researched and visited dozens of countries, but his specialties are Ireland, Prague, Hawaii and the U.S. Southeast. That said, we love a challenge — tell us where you want to go and we’ll dive into the data.

How long does it take to receive my itinerary?

It depends on the package you select. For Budget Explorer and Lite plans, we turn around your research within 72 hours. Concierge and Group plans may take a bit longer because we handle the bookings and negotiations, but you’ll always receive an initial proposal within 4 business days. Need it sooner? Ask about rush service.

What is the Value Score?

The Value Score is our way of quantifying how much money we save you compared to typical retail prices. We look at historical averages for flights, lodging and cars, then calculate a score based on the percentage saved. A higher Value Score means you’re getting more trip for your money.

Are planning fees refundable?

Planning fees cover the time and research required to uncover the best deals. If we can’t provide at least two flight and lodging options within your stated budget, we’ll credit your payment toward a future trip or refund 50%, your choice. Otherwise, planning fees are non‑refundable once research begins.

What do you handle for each package?

Our service tiers correspond to different levels of involvement. For Budget Explorer and Lite plans we focus on research and inspiration: we analyse prices and destinations for you, compile two to three itineraries and send you booking links so you can finalise the trip yourself. Concierge clients receive hands‑on planning: we search, negotiate and book flights, lodging and cars using your payment information and deliver a complete travel packet. Group and Premium projects include all of the above plus coordination among travellers, payment splitting, activity reservations and real‑time support during your trip.
