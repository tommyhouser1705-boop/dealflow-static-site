How Flip Flop Travel Vouchers Work

  1. You buy the voucher. You’ll receive a confirmation email, and I’ll send a personalized digital voucher (PDF) you can forward or print.
  2. They redeem it online. The recipient fills out a quick trip request form and enters their voucher code.
  3. I plan the trip. I hunt down the best deals that match their budget and package type, and deliver everything in a clear, easy-to-use format.

Fine Print (In Plain English)

  • Vouchers are valid for 12 months from purchase.
  • Voucher covers my planning/research/booking fee, not the actual cost of flights, hotels, or rentals.
  • Pricing is per traveler. For groups, multiple vouchers can be applied, and group discounts may be available.
  • Vouchers are non-refundable but can be transferred once (for example, if your friend can’t travel, they can give it to someone else).
  • All trips are subject to airline, hotel, and provider availability at the time of booking — deals change fast, and flexibility helps a lot.
