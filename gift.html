How Flip Flop Travel Vouchers Work

  1. You buy the voucher. You’ll receive a confirmation email, and I’ll send a personalized digital voucher you can forward or print — perfect to slip into a card or gift box.
  2. They redeem it online. The recipient fills out a quick trip request form, enters their voucher code, and tells me their budget, dates, and preferences.
  3. I plan the trip. I hunt down the best deals that match their budget and package type, and deliver everything in a clear, easy-to-use format with next steps.

Whether it’s a Christmas surprise, graduation gift, or a “you deserve a break” present, you’re giving something they’ll actually remember: time away and real memories.

Fine Print (Simple & Fair)

  • Vouchers are valid for 12 months from purchase.
  • Voucher covers my planning, research, and/or booking fee, not the actual cost of flights, hotels, or rentals.
  • Pricing is per traveler. For groups, multiple vouchers can be applied, and group discounts may be available.
  • Vouchers are non-refundable but can be transferred once (for example, if your friend can’t travel, they can give it to someone else).
  • All trips are subject to airline, hotel, and provider availability at the time of booking — deals change fast, and flexibility helps a lot.
