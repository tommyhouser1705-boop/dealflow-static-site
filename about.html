Meet Tommy

I’m Tommy — a college student who refuses to let a tight budget get in the way of exploring the world. Over the past few years I’ve visited 14 countries and countless U.S. destinations, all while studying full‑time. My favorite places include Ireland, Prague and Hawaii. I spend hours digging into flight charts, lodging deals, rental car loopholes and anything else that makes travel cheaper.

Flip Flop Travel Co is the result of that obsession. I started sharing my itineraries with friends and realized there was a demand for honest, data‑driven trip planning. Now I’m bringing those tools and tricks to you — because everyone deserves a dream vacation without the markup.

Let’s Plan Your Story

I’ve turned my personal experiences into a system that works. If you’re ready to travel without overpaying, We’d love to help you write your own travel story.
